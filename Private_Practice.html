Here are some of the data science projects I have taken up in my spare time.

World Economic Indicators

The analysis of global economic indicators, based on World Bank and United Nations data from 1960 to 2021, highlights key trends in economic growth, population dynamics, and human development.
The project involved cleaning and standardizing the dataset, followed by advanced visualizations using Power BI to reveal significant patterns. The analysis uncovered a steady increase in population density and life expectancy, with notable GDP growth between 2001 and 2007.
Regions such as Europe and Central Asia dominated in terms of GDP per capita, while South Asia showed the highest Human Development Index (HDI) growth in the 21st century.

The insights indicate a strong link between GDP and quality of life, emphasizing the economic and social disparities across regions. Most countries fall into the middle-income bracket, with variations in unemployment and economic performance evident.
Notably, income data and population growth suggest potential investment opportunities in fast-growing regions. Overall, the analysis underscores the complex relationship between economic and human development, aiding policy formulation and long-term planning for sustainable growth.

Tool Used: Power BI.

UK Railway

The analysis of the UK rail system for National Rail Transport, covering January to April 2024, identified key patterns in passenger behavior and operational efficiency.
Using Power BI, I created interactive dashboards that highlighted popular routes, peak travel times, revenue trends, and on-time performance. Key findings included a preference for online ticket purchases, with peak travel periods during morning (6 AM – 8 AM) and evening (4 PM – 6 PM) rush hours
High-demand routes, such as Manchester Piccadilly to Liverpool Lime Street, required better resource allocation, and significant revenue was generated from advance tickets and standard class options.

Recommendations focused on increasing capacity for popular routes, adjusting schedules for peak travel periods, revising pricing strategies for key routes, and improving infrastructure to reduce delays and cancellations.

Tool Used: Power BI.

Road Accident Analysis

This project focuses on analyzing road accident data using Power BI, incorporating a comprehensive methodology that includes data cleaning, processing, modeling, and integration with a CSV file.
The dataset includes records of road accidents and statistics related to road users, providing insights into the factors influencing these incidents.
By examining variables such as weather conditions, time, and location, the analysis aims to identify trends and contributing factors, particularly in relation to different types of vehicles.

Additionally, the project explores the demographics of traffic accident victims across various countries to identify patterns and differences.
The objective is to determine if there are consistent trends in accident demographics, which could inform recommendations for a targeted action plan to improve road safety.

Tool Used: SQL, Power BI.

Maji Ndogo Water Services

This data analysis aims to provide insights into the progress of the water services of a fictitious country, Maji Ndogo, over the past year.
The goal was to identify challenges faced by communities and assess the effectiveness of the personnel responsible for water services. Phase 1 centered on cleaning and aggregating data using SQL to reveal underlying issues with water sources, facilitating actionable insights.
Phase 2 focused on identifying corruption by comparing water quality data, identifying inconsistencies, and analyzing employee errors, which uncovered potential corrupt practices during water surveys. This phase emphasized using SQL functions, including CTEs, subqueries, and views.

Key findings led to several targeted recommendations: deploying temporary water trucks for river-dependent communities, installing filters in polluted wells, optimizing shared tap usage, and addressing infrastructure repairs with significant reach.
Additionally, allegations of corruption were raised against four officials based on error analysis, highlighting the need for accountability in water quality assessments. This project not only addressed technical challenges but also reinforced MySQL skills in data cleaning and analysis.

Tool Used: SQL.
